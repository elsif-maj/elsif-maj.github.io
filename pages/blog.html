I'm Still Studying (2-12-22)

I passed the first part of the first Launchschool exam a few weeks back, and I have since been studying a little bit each day for the second half of the exam. These first exams -- covering nothing less than 'Programming Foundations' -- test ability over a wide possible range of contents.

Because I came to the craft of programming without having any background (just a few months ago,) and because these initial tests are so substantial, I have found myself having to answer a call to patience for the present time. Each day I practice the craft a bit and I allow time and consistent effort to calcify my new abilities.

The practice of writing code is itself inherently satisfying, and so is the confidence gained from knowing that I will not move forward to new concepts until I have mastered the ones at hand. I feel liberated from having to design my study towards clearing a bar by a deadline and can instead focus on genuinely transforming my capacities.

Update (1-27-22)

It's unfortunate that I have not posted anything on this blog since its inception -- but I am very much having to triage the things I would like to do with my time.

I have decided to orchestrate an exit from the full-time work world. This is due in part to a rare contentment and satisfaction I find in the practice of programming and in part due to a certainty that I must not make a lengthy career of my current job. I am lucky to be in a position where I should be able to live frugally for a year or two without having to work, and commit myself totally to a reinvention of my work-life and -self.

In the meantime I am fixing up my sailboat to put it on the market, working the full time job that was 'too much' even when I did nothing with my off hours, making sure to do some coding exercises every night, studying for the second Launschool exam, hunting for an apartment in SF, and trying to cook food at home and exercise every day.

I'll try to include some blogging from time to time, but frankly it's only a footnote on my present list of priorities!
