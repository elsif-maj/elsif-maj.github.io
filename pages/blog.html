Update (1-27-22)

It's unfortunate that I have not posted anything on this blog since it's inception -- but I am very much having to triage the things I would like to do with my time.

I have decided to orchestrate an exit from the full-time work world. This is due in part to a rare contentment and satisfaction I find in the practice of programming and in part due to a certainty that I must not make a lengthy career of my current job. I am lucky to be in a position where I should be able to live frugally for a year or two without having to work, and commit myself totally to a reinvention of my work life and self.

In the meantime I am fixing up my sailboat to put it on the market, working the full time job that was 'too much' even when I did nothing with my off hours, making sure to do some coding exercises every night, studying for the second Launschool exam, hunting for an apartment in SF, and trying to cook food at home and exercise every day.

I'll try to include some blogging from time to time, but frankly its only a footnote on my present list of priorities!
