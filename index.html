Umbra is a browser app that prioritizes making real-time collaboration on code as quick, painless, and convenient as possible. It occupies a middle-ground between heavy-weight and full-featured IDE multi-user collaboration tools, and the more convenient and lightweight code-sharing tools that may not offer runnable code, or real-time collaboration, or that may require user-signin or clicking through multiple views before a collaborative session can be initiated and shared with someone.
